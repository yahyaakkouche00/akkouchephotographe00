Fagradalsfjall
from US$149.00 / 1490.00 DHS

When the Fagradalsfjall Volcano Erupted in the spring of 2021, it captured much attention both in Iceland and around the world. It was the first time a volcano erupted on the Reykjanes Peninsula in 815 years and even though there had been weeks of earthquakes and seismic activity, it caught almost everyone by surprise.

It happened during the height of the Covid-19 Pandemic and turned out to be a very safe and easily accessible eruption. Located just 1 hour drive from Reykjavik followed by a refreshing hike. It was visited by all generations in Iceland and at times became an incredible communal gathering place - like gathering around a bonfire - just much bigger and more breathtaking. The Fagradalsfjall eruption will forever be remembered by everyone who was in Iceland at that time.
